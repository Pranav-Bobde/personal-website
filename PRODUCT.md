# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People who want to understand Pranav Bobde's work, writing, and technical taste: founders, engineering teams, AI builders, peers, and readers coming from blog or video links.

## Product Purpose

This site is Pranav's personal publishing and credibility surface. It should make the work easy to browse, make technical ideas feel direct and practical, and route interested people toward blogs, videos, code, social links, or contact.

## Brand Personality

Direct, technical, slightly raw. The site should feel like a personal terminal and publishing desk, not a corporate portfolio template.

## Anti-references

Avoid glossy SaaS landing-page polish, decorative card-heavy layouts, generic AI-gradient visuals, over-explained UI copy, and anything that makes the voice feel outsourced or consultant-polished.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the signal dense: every section should help a visitor understand the person, work, or idea faster.
2. Preserve the terminal rhythm: compact type, sharp borders, keyboard hints, and restrained motion.
3. Make publishing surfaces easy to scan first, then easy to read or watch deeply.
4. Let personal voice stay visible; do not smooth everything into neutral brand copy.
5. Use media when it helps the content travel, but keep it grounded in the existing black/cyan system.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain high contrast, keyboard-friendly navigation, visible focus states, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion-safe interactions. Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
